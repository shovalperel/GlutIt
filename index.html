How it works

Safe eating in three steps

Step 1

Search nearby

Type a dish, cuisine, or restaurant name. GlutIt searches places reported by the GF community around you.

Step 2

Check GF confidence

Each place shows a confidence score based on community reports — dedicated kitchens, cross-contamination risk, and more.

Step 3

Go eat, report back

Navigate directly to the restaurant. After your visit, leave a report to help others in the community stay safe.

Features

Everything you need to eat safely

GF Confidence Score

A live score computed from community reports, updated after every visit.

Live Map View

See all GF-friendly spots on a map pinned around your current location.

Community Reports

Real experiences from people with celiac disease and gluten sensitivity — not just menu claims.

Safety Alerts

Get notified when a place you've bookmarked receives new cross-contamination reports.
